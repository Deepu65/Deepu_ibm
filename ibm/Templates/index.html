DIAZINON

Diazinon functions as an acetylcholinesterase (AChE) inhibitor. This enzyme breaks down the neurotransmitter acetylcholine (ACh) into choline and an acetate group.[7] The inhibition of AChE causes an abnormal accumulation of ACh in the synaptic cleft.[citation needed] When diazinon enters the body, it is oxidatively decomposed to diazoxon, an organophosphate compound that is much more poisonous than diazinon; it mainly causes the inhibition of AChE.[8] The conversion of diazinon to diazoxon (Reaction 1) is performed by the liver microsomal enzyme system and requires O2 and NADPH

BLOOM BUDDY

Organic growth boosters can be used in a variety of ways, depending on the type of growth booster and the plant’s needs. Here are some common application methods: Soil Drench: Mix the organic growth booster with water and apply it to the soil around the base of the plant. This method is effective for providing a slow-release of nutrients to the plant’s roots. Foliar Spray: Mix the organic growth booster with water and apply it to the plant’s leaves. This method is effective for providing a quick boost of nutrients to the plant and improving its overall health. Top Dressing: Sprinkle the organic growth booster over the soil around the base of the plant. This method is effective for providing a slow-release of nutrients to the plant’s roots.
